## Building the Map Deck
Deep Dark 100 Dice (DD1D) is modeled after a dungeon crawl, and at the table IRL the "dungeon rooms" would be laid out like the hours of a clock using tarot cards from a **Map Deck**. The digital companion constructs the Map Deck automatically, but if you'd like to play at home you can build your own by:
1. Putting nine random Major Arcana cards into a face-down pile and shuffle.
2. Putting one random card from each Minor Arcana suit into a second face-down pile and shuffle.
3. Deal out the nine Major Arcana into four piles, leaving the last card in reserve.
4. Deal out one of the four Minor Arcana cards onto each of the four piles.
5. Shuffle each of the four piles, then stack them up and place those 12 cards on top of the last card in reserve.
<br><br>
You should now have a stack of 13 cards, with one Minor Arcana card every 3 cards or so. This is the Map Deck that you'll use for this game, but not others. The Deep Woods shift and transmute in unpredictable ways, so no two games are the same.
